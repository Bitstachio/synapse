# Project Objectives

This project was guided by the following specific objectives:

- To identify and characterize the Micro-Alignment Gap in current AI safety frameworks as it pertains to individual employee well-being, privacy and security.
- To develop a governance framework that integrates ethical psychological safety and human autonomy as core AI safety features
- To produce a system that can be integrated and introduced into any company’s emerging technologies and can be used how their framework would require.
- To design a human-in-command solution that uses AI to ensure ethical guidelines are followed maintaining employee well-being.
