# Rationale and Design Principles

The design of the solution is guided by several key principles:

- **Human-Centered Design:** Prioritizing usability and clarity for employees
- **Transparency:** Ensuring users understand why decisions are made
- **Efficiency:** Reducing the time and effort required to interpret policies
- **Trust:** Building confidence in AI systems through guidance and support

Rather than enforcing compliance through restriction, the system enables employees to make informed decisions.
