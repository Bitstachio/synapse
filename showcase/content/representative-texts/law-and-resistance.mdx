# Law and Resistance

## Resisting Meta Content Moderation

**Citation:** Mbioh, W. R. (2025). Resisting Meta: Content moderation, diffraction and the constitutive power of Kenyan law within the Global South. International Journal of Law in Context, 21(4), 711–729. https://doi.org/10.1017/S1744552325100153

**Rationale:** This peer‑reviewed article examines the legal and political context of Meta’s content moderation operations in Kenya. It explains how global technology companies outsource digital labor to the Global South and how Kenyan law and activism are responding to these practices.
