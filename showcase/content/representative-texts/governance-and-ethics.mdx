# Governance and Ethics

## AI Labor Governance

**Citation:** Walle, Y. M. (2025). Breaking digital silence: AI, structural bias, and the uneven landscape of whistleblowing. AI & Society. Advance online publication. https://doi.org/10.1007/s00146-025-02758-0

**Rationale:** This peer reviewed article discusses global governance challenges related to AI systems and digital labor markets. It analyzes how technology companies rely on outsourced workers to label data, train algorithms, and moderate online content.
