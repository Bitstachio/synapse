# Psychology and Labor

## AI Labor and Emotional Labor

**Citation:** Faniyi, O. (2026). A killjoy witness: African content moderators and AI’s relationalities of extraction. Women’s Studies in Communication, 49(1), 189–192. https://doi.org/10.1080/07491409.2025.2605934

**Rationale:** This peer‑reviewed study explores the emotional demands placed on workers who moderate harmful online content. It highlights how repeated exposure to disturbing material can lead to psychological distress and trauma among content moderators.
