# AI & The Transformation of Labor

## The Hidden Cost of Content Moderation in Kenya

**Summary of Research:** Our project examines the labour conditions of Kenyan content moderators who review graphic and disturbing online material to train AI systems. We argue that these systems rely on a "surrogate humanity" in the Global South, where workers experience psychological harm and low wages while maintaining the illusion that AI moderation systems operate automatically.
