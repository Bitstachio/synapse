# Analysis and Interpretation

The proposed website solution demonstrates several notable strengths, particularly in its ability to turn complex and often inaccessible policy frameworks into clear, actionable guidance for users. By reducing cognitive load and presenting information in a more user-friendly format, the system enhances usability and supports more confident decision-making.

The integration of AI-driven assistance further strengthens this approach by providing immediate, context-specific feedback, allowing users to identify and address issues in real time rather than navigating lengthy documentation independently, saving time and reducing user errors.

Despite these strengths, certain limitations remain. AI-generated suggestions may not always capture the full contextual nuances of a user’s situation, which can affect the relevance or precision of the recommendations. In addition, the system’s overall effectiveness is closely tied to the accuracy, completeness, and ongoing maintenance of the underlying framework. As such, further validation through real-world user testing is necessary to assess its reliability and practical impact in workplace environments. Thus, it is important to back up the framework in regular intervals for maximum accuracy.

A key insight that emerged from this project is that employees tend to value clarity, guidance, and support over rigid control mechanisms. Rather than seeking strict enforcement, users benefit more from systems that help them understand why certain actions may be problematic and how to improve them. This finding shows the importance of adopting a human-centered approach to AI design, one that prioritizes accessibility, transparency, and user empowerment alongside technical and regulatory considerations.
